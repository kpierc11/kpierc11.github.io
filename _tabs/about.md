Hi all - My name is Kaleb Pierce and I've been a professional web developer for the past 5 years. I've worked with several different web platforms such as WordPress and many programming languages and frameworks.

The reason I started programming, as most people I'm sure say, was to develop video games. I was previously a trumpet player and was planning on being a music teacher or professional player in a symphony. After sitting in one of the practice rooms after a long session, for the first time I really felt as if this wasn't what I should be doing. I didn't enjoy it anymore. The pressure and anxiety of constant performances and long practice hours sapped all of my passion for being a musician. I knew I needed to pursue a different path.

I took some time to self reflect and asked myself what do I spend most of my time doing? Playing games of course! I thought since I enjoy playing them, talking about them and doing deep dives into their lore and stories, that I would love to make them. I started to buy books and watched intro game dev videos.

Trying to learn how to make games proved to be extremely difficult. I made some small progress and dabbled in several game engines but still haven't completed a full game that I would be satisfied with. I'm still left with unfinished game projects in Godot, Unreal Engine and SDL3 . I decided to switch gears for a few years and focus on a career in web development. Game development jobs are extremely completive and there are many talented individuals out there. So here I am now 30 and I'm ready to start again and give it all I've got!
